COM Events

The application of student activities at the College of Medicine and allows the staff and students of the College of Medicine to: - Review and learn about the activities of the College of Medicine - Availability to register for the announced events for students of the College of Medicine - Obtaining the necessary approvals to hold the events under the supervision of the College of Medicine - Obtaining an official letter stating the approval of the event - Issuance of certificates for male and female students participating in the activities held
